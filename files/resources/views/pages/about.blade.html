About

We're the calm ones in a loud industry

Lumen started in 2025 after our founder spent a year watching her support team demo tools that promised everything and shipped confusion. We do the opposite: small pilots, real baselines, honest math, and a hard rule that humans own the judgment calls.

01

02

People keep the judgment
Assistants draft, summarize, and route. Humans decide. Every deployment ships with clear escalation paths and a visible off switch.

03

Leave teams stronger
Success is your team running the system without us. Documentation and training aren't add-ons; they're the deliverable.
