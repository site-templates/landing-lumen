Case study · Harborline

A support team that stopped drowning

The problem. A 14-person support team answering 9,000 tickets a month, with weekend backlogs regularly topping 600 and CSAT slipping quarter over quarter.

The pilot. An assistant that drafts replies for the 20 most common ticket types, grounded in Harborline's own help center — with every draft reviewed by an agent before sending.

The result. Six months later the same team handles 40% more volume, weekends clear by Saturday noon, and the two agents who hated ticket triage now run the knowledge base.
